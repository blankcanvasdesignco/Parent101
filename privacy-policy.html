Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 October 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how "Parent101" by Blank Canvas Designs Private Limited handles and protects your personal data. By using the app, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described below.

1. General Information

"Parent101" is an iOS application designed to provide parenting advice and tools, including a chat feature with AI-driven responses informed by child psychology. However, the app is not a substitute for professional medical advice. We do not store any personal data on external servers; all data remains on your device.

2. Data Collection and Transmission

Parent101 operates locally on your device, meaning no personal data, chat history, or user inputs are stored externally. However, for the AI-driven chat feature, input data (e.g., text from your queries) is transmitted to OpenAI’s servers to generate responses. This data is processed solely for the purpose of providing parenting-related advice and is not stored by Blank Canvas Designs Private Limited or OpenAI.

3. Data Retention

We do not retain any of your personal data. All data generated within the app remains stored on your device. If you delete the app, all associated data will be permanently deleted from your device. Data transmitted to OpenAI is processed in real-time to generate responses and is not retained for future use.

4. User Control and Data Deletion

You maintain full control over the data stored on your device. Deleting the app from your iPhone will permanently delete all associated data. Since no data is stored externally, removing the app deletes all your data.

5. Third-Party Data Processing (OpenAI)

The AI chat feature in Parent101 is powered by OpenAI’s services. Data provided to OpenAI is processed only to generate AI responses to your input and is not stored or reused. We encourage you to review OpenAI’s privacy policy for more information on their data practices.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for use by parents and caregivers, and is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or store any data from individuals under 13. If we become aware that such data has been inadvertently provided, it will be promptly deleted.

7. Disclaimer on Medical Advice

While Parent101 is built with input from child psychologists, it is not intended as a source of medical advice. The information provided by the AI chat is for guidance purposes only and should not be considered a substitute for consulting a licensed child psychologist or medical professional. Always seek professional medical advice for any concerns regarding your child’s well-being.

8. Security

We follow best practices for security within the iOS environment. We recommend users take standard precautions, such as using strong passwords and enabling device security features, to protect their personal data on the device.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any significant changes through an update in the app. The latest version of the Privacy Policy will always be available within the app and on our website.
